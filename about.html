About

St. Joseph’s First Grade College is one of the pioneering institutions under MDES (Mysore Diocesan Educational Society) that has been rendering yeomen service in the field of education. With more than five decades of experience in transforming young minds, the institution caters to students from nursery to undergraduate courses.

St. Joseph’s First Grade College, Jayalakshmipuram, Mysore affiliated to the University of Mysore was established on 18th July, 2011. With nearly a decade’s experience in offering UG programs, the institution is presently home to 1000 plus students, a dedicated team of 50 teachers and dynamic principal. The institution is highly focused and dedicated to its motto of transfiguring young minds.
